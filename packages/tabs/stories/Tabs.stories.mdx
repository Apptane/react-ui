## Appearance

You can modify `spacing`, `offset` and `alignment` and enable `border` to customize appearance.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Appearance">
    <StateManager initialValue={3}>
      {({ state, setState }) => (
        <Tabs size="large" value={state} onChange={setState} border spacing={4} offset={50} alignment="left">
          <Tabs.Item value={1}>One</Tabs.Item>
          <Tabs.Item value={2}>Two</Tabs.Item>
          <Tabs.Item value={3}>Three</Tabs.Item>
        </Tabs>
      )}
    </StateManager>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

Child elements that are not of `TabItem` type are appended as custom content to the right of the tab items.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Custom">

          <Tabs.Item value={1}>

              One

            </Pane>
          </Tabs.Item>
          <Tabs.Item value={2}>Two</Tabs.Item>
          <Tabs.Item value={3}>Three</Tabs.Item>
          <Pane verticalAlignment="center" marginLeft={16}>
            <IconButton iconName="i:close" size="small" />
